About Us

"Suleimania Egypt for Food Industries & Export" is a leading agricultural and industrial fortress specializing in the processing, pickling, and exporting of the finest Egyptian table olives to global markets. From our state-of-the-art facilities on the Wadi El Natrun - El Alamein Road, we provide our partners with a premium selection including: Azizy, Picual, Manzanillo, Kalamata, and Dulce olives.
